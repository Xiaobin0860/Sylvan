Detailed Description

An internal chessboard class.

This is the base class for all chess variants. Board's main purposes are:

  • keeping track of the game, and not just piece positions, but also castling rights, en passant square, played moves, repetitions, etc.
  • verifying the legality of moves played
  • parsing and generating strings for moves and positions in notations such as FEN, SAN, and Coordinate notation
  • providing information about a game to a graphical board widget
